Amino acids are the monomers from which proteins are made. Amino acids contain an amino group - NH2, carboxylic acid group and a variable R group which is a carbon-containing chain. There are 20 different amino acids with different R groups. Amino acids are joined by peptide bonds formed in condensation reactions. A dipeptide contains 2 amino acids and polypeptides contain 3 or more amino acids.
